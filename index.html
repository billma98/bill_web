About Me

Control Engineer at Shift Robotics with a background in Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, and Control Systems. A Carnegie Mellon University Master's graduate in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in the development of control systems for robotic lower limb prosthesis OSL (Open Source Leg). Expertise includes applying SLAM and computer vision to enhance motion control in robotic prostheses. Open to professional networking, discussions, and sharing insights within the industry.
