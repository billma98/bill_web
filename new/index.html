ABOUT

Developed Real Time Controllers for Prosthetics and Robotic Shoes

Founding Control Engineer at Shift Robotics with expertise in Robotics and Control Systems. I led the development of the first three major control system versions (OS), largely enhancing product safety, responsiveness, and performance. I also introduced customizable control settings, enabling users to fine-tune their experience via a phone app. My code has been integrated into the final manufactured product, delivered to end users and partner companies.

Master's in Mechanical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, specializing in control systems for robotic lower limb prostheses (Open Source Leg). ShiftOS 3.0

  • ShiftOS 3.0 introduces iOS App connectivity for ensuring an intuitive and tailored experience.
  • Includes a One-Step OTA Experience, significantly saving users time and steps.
  • Provides a Real-Time Dashboard for monitoring and understanding both software and hardware information.
  • Features well-designed Preset Profiles offering various acceleration, deceleration, and top-speed combinations tailored for different scenarios: Indoor, City, and Sport modes.
  • Introduces Advanced Settings Toggles for personalized profiles, allowing users to fine-tune features and controller values to suit their preferences.
  • Delivers enhanced responsiveness, a 50% range boost, and improved safety features for smoother, safer, and more efficient walking.

ShiftOS 2.1

  • ShiftOS 2.1 introduced the innovative Calibration Mode, enabling personalized gait calibration for a smoother, more tailored walking experience.
  • Users simply walk naturally for 12-15 consistent steps, allowing the shoes to analyze, calculate, and save their unique gait patterns.
  • Reduces false or missed detections by 83%in uneven, bumpy terrains or complex environments. Eliminates sudden stops caused by challenging ground conditions and chaotic gait motions.

ShiftOS 2.0

  • ShiftOS 2.0 enhanced gait detection & safety, 70% fewer false detections for different user Gait Motions. Improved controller's robustness and safety to prevent accidents.
  • TRAINING Mode: Caps speed and acceleration for smooth onboarding, helping users learn and train with ease.
  • Improved Performance: Reduces jerk, cuts time to top speed by 30%, and increases initial acceleration by 50% for better support from the first step.

ShiftOS 1.0

  • ShiftOS 1.0 enables OTA updates via the app.
  • Introduces speed derating for better performance.
