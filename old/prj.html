ShiftOS

ShiftOS 1.0: The First Step Toward Smarter Walking

  • ShiftOS 1.0 enables OTA updates via the app, allowing phones to connect seamlessly with the shoes.
  • It also introduces speed derating to enhance durability and optimize performance during summer usage.

ShiftOS 2.0: Smarter, Safer, and More Personalized

  • ShiftOS 2.0 introduces enhanced gait detection with 83% fewer false detections, smoother acceleration/deceleration, and improved safety protocols to prevent accidents.
  • The new Calibration Mode in ShiftOS 2.1 allows personalized gait calibration for a more tailored and seamless walking experience.

ShiftOS 3.0: Seamless, Efficient, and Highly Customizable Controller

  • ShiftOS 3.0 introduces iOS App connectivity for advanced settings, real-time data, and personalized profiles, ensuring an intuitive and tailored experience.
  • Enhanced responsiveness, a 50% range boost, and improved safety features make every step smoother, safer, and more efficient.
